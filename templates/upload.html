Image Guidelines
Do
  • Clear, front-facing face photos
  • Good lighting (natural or indoor)
  • 20–50 images per student
  • Vary angles slightly (left, right, up, down)
  • Different lighting conditions
  • Image size ≥ 200×200 px
Avoid
  • Blurry or dark images
  • Faces partially covered (mask, sunglasses)
  • Fewer than 10 images per student
  • Images with multiple faces
Pipeline:
  1. MTCNN detects & aligns faces → dataset/aligned/
  2. FaceNet extracts 512-D embeddings
  3. SVM classifier trains on embeddings
  4. Classifier saved to model/classifier.pkl
